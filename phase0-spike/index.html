How to run the full test

1. In Safari: Share button, then "Add to Home Screen". Open from the icon, not Safari.
2. Play the story, take a photo.
3. Airplane mode ON. Close the app fully (swipe away).
4. Reopen from the icon: story must play, photo must still be there.
5. Flip the silent switch and play again: audio must sound.
Poseidon is watching. No signal required.
